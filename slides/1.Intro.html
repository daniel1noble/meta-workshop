What is meta-analysis?

  • Statistical procedure for synthesising effect size results from different studies
  • Part of a systematic review
    • Transparent and reproducible literature searches
    • Reproducible inclusion and exclusion criteria

  • Identifies gaps and biases in existing research
  • Explore why effects vary across studies
  • Can test specific hypotheses
